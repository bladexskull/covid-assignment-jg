DO's And Dont's
Do✔️ Wash your hands regularly for 20 seconds, with soap and water or alcohol-based hand rub ✋ 🧼 🚰
Do✔️Cover your nose and mouth with a disposable tissue or flexed elbow when you cough or sneeze 🤧 👃 💪
Do✔️Avoid close contact (1 meter or 3 feet) with people who are unwell 🙏 😷 🚶‍
Do✔️ Stay home and self-isolate from others in the household if you feel unwell 🏠 👨 💉
Don❌ t Touch your eyes, nose, or mouth if your hands are not clean 👃 👁️ 🤭
